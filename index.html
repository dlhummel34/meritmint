Our Commitment to Personal Information Protection

 

“Privacy Policy” for Nihonsakari Co., Ltd

Under our management philosophy of fostering “Dreams for enterprises, happiness for society, and a Nihonsakari loved by people, we are engaged mainly in manufacturing and distribution of sake and other relevant business in accordance with our creed “Quality first, reputation first, implementation first”. For this reason, we will require our employees to be fully aware of the importance of protection of personal information processed by the Company (hereinafter referred to as “Personal Information”), and prescribe internal regulations for Personal Information control to promote its implementation, maintenance, and improvement in a consistent manner.

  1. The Company will comply with the applicable laws and regulations, guidance provided by the Government, or other relevant standards for Personal Information processed at all our businesses as well as Personal Information of employees, and establish a Personal Information Protection Management System in compliance with the “Requirements for Personal Information Protection Management System” pursuant to Japanese Industrial Standards (JIS Q15001) to protect your Personal Information.
  2. In collecting and using Personal Information, we will specify the purposes of use of Personal Information, and process Personal Information to the extent necessary for accomplishing such specified purposes, and take appropriate measures to avoid any use other than such specified purposes (“Use for an Unintended Purpose”).
  3. Personal Information collected by us will not be provided or disclosed to any third party unless otherwise allowable by the applicable laws and regulations or with consent of the principal.
  4. Personal Information collected by us will be accumulated, maintained, and strictly controlled in a safe manner in order to protect unauthorized disclosure, loss, or destruction.
  5. Upon request from a customer for disclosure, correction, or deletion of Personal Information, the following contact for inquiries will respond to your request.
  6. We will perform ongoing improvements of the Personal Information Protection Management System.

1.Definition of Personal Information

The term Personal Information means “Information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify an individual, such as name, birthday, or other statement, or number, symbol, or code that is assigned to an individual, which are included in such information. Personal Information may include personally identifiable information that;

i.is acquired via toll free call, fax, post card, internet, or email;

ii.is acquired from an application for campaigns, or answers to a questionnaire;

iii.is made available to us during the ordinary course of business with customers (such as purchase history, or use by customers); and

iv.is acquired from an entry sheet or resume collected through the recruitment process

 

2.Acquisition of Personal Information

In acquiring Personal Information, we will specify the purpose for acquiring your Personal Information via a statement in writing (such as an application), from a website, or verbally, in a lawful and fair manner. The telephone conversation may be recorded for the purpose of correctly understanding an order, opinion, request, or inquiry from you, and ongoing improvement of our service quality.

 

3.Voluntary acceptance of acquisition of Personal Information

Entry or input of your Personal Information will be voluntarily made; however, if you do not enter your Personal Information into a written or online form indicating “This information must be entered”, your request may not be accommodated.

 

4.Purpose of Personal Information

Unless otherwise required by the applicable laws and regulations, Personal Information will be used to the extent necessary to accomplish the purpose hereof and will be subject to your consent; provided, however, that Personal Information may be used to:

i.deliver goods ordered by you, or send direct mail advertising catalogs for goods to be provided by us;

ii.describe the method of use or provide the relevant information upon inquiries prior to your purchase of goods;

iii.administer an aggregate questionnaire, campaign, or prize competition;

iv.provide guidance for recruitment or determine whether to hire an employee (Personal Information pertinent to recruitment may not be used for direct mail advertising purposes);

v.carry out and facilitate work in whole or in part entrusted by other providers to process Personal Information, in an appropriate manner.

vi.control and administer the hiring of employees;

 

5.Entrustment

Your Personal Information may not be entrusted to any third party (excluding external providers who executed binding confidential agreements to protect Personal Information) except to the extent necessary to accomplish the purpose hereof. Permitted entrustment includes;

i.to deliver goods ordered by you, or entrust delivery to home delivery services; and

ii.to deliver catalogs or direct mail, or entrust the making of address labels to a printer

 

6.Disclosure, correction, and deletion of Personal Information

If you wish to have the Company disclose or correct your Personal Information, we will reasonably respond to your request in a prompt manner. For any inquiry or correction, our Customer Service handling Personal Information will be consulted. The Company may invoice you those charges on actuals for disclosure. In principle, we do not accept any request for disclosure or correction from a family member (excluding those living together for which the Company determines that it is necessary to do so in accordance with a purchase agreement).

 

7.Consultation and claims for processing of Personal Information
